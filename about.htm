ABOUT

Open Art is based in Southsea, Hampshire and was founded by a group of friends who were keen to use their practice to promote creativity in their community and amplify the voices of less represented groups. Between them were writers, theatre makers, musicians and designers.
They are currently applying for ACE funding to increase the scope of Open Art.
